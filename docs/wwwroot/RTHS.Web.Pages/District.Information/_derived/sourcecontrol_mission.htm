Mission Statement

Richardton-Taylor  Public School District #4 will provide the educational opportunities necessary for every student to achieve and succeed within his/her capacity to learn and progress.  These educational opportunities will stress the necessity of lifelong learning for each student and make every effort to prepare the student for the challenges of the 21st century.

Richardton-Taylor 
High School

Richardton Public Schools formed a coop with Taylor in 1983.  Then in 2001 the two districts officially merged.  Today 171 students in grades 7-12 attend school in Richardton with 126 K-6 students at Taylor.   Richardton-Taylor High School has been a leader in computer and technology classes along with strong programs in all curricular areas.  Every classroom is on the school computer network with internet access for all teachers and students.
