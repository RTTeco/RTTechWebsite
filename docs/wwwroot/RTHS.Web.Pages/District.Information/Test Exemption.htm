Test Exemption

2007-2008

 

 

1st Semester – No test exemption.

            I think that everyone needs to take a semester test at least once a year. Some college students do not think that high school prepared them for the tests in college. I agree with that statement.

 

2nd Semester – Your test exemption is based on the following criteria. This is a class by class bases.

 

            Have to have a B or better in the class and not have missed more than 3 days of school.

 

            Freshman and Sophomores will take semester tests in the four core areas:

                        English, Math, Science, and Social Studies

 

            If you receive an advanced proficient on the state assessment and miss 3 or less school days per semester you are exempt from all semester test for the 2nd semester.
