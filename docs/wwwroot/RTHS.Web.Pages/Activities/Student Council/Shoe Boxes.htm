Student Council Shoeboxes

 

The RTHS Student Council participated in the program Operation Christmas Child again this year. This marks the 10th year that RTHS has participated in the program.

Operation Christmas Child is a non-denominational program that targets children who live in poverty and in war-torn countries.  This year, millions of children will experience Christmas joy when they recieve shoe boxes filled with gifts.

If you wish to fill a shoe box, these are some gift suggestions that the RTHS Student Council used in 2008,
