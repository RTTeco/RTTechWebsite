Cloning Project
Cloning of Dolly http://www.synapses.co.uk/science/clone.html This website tells about how scientists were able to successfully clone a sheep. This site also tells why cloning is being done. This site also has pictures to help explain the process of cloning.
How Cloning Works http://howstuffworks.lycoszone.com/cloning.htm This site will tell you about other animals that have been cloned since the sheep. This site will tell you how cloning is done and some of the risks involved. It also tells how scientists cloned an endangered gaur, or Indian ox, to help save the species.
How Human Cloning Will Work http://howstuffworks.lycoszone.com/human-cloning.htm This site tells how human cloning is possible. This site will tell you how many organizations are already begining the study of cloning human cells. Also this tells how human cloning can be dangerous to people.
