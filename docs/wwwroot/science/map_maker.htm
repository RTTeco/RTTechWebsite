Map Maker

Directions:

 After reading the following directions, click on the map maker link button and follow the directions to make your map.

1. Set the minimum Bounding rectangle with your Ist longitude then your next longitude followed by your 1st latitude then your next latitude.

2. Set your map width in inches.

3. For now ignore the map projection boxes.

4. Choose the plot feature.

5. Title your map.

Set these for your first map.

1. Longitude and latitude... 240/300/20/50

2. Map width.... 10

3. Plot features.....Political Boundaries

Click on Plot Map, then answer the following questions.
