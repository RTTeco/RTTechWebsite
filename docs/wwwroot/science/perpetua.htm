Rube Goldberg Project

After completing the questions above, begin the design of your machine. You will need to have a minimum of 10 energy transfers within the machine and each transfer will have to complete some specific task.....for example if your machine turns on a light bulb the light must do something such as trigger a photocell. As an end result, your machine must complete one of the following;

a) Raise a flag up a flag pole

b) Ring a door bell

c) Blow out a candle

d) Pop a balloon

e) Un-zip a zipper

f) Lock a ziplock bag

g) Flip a pancake
