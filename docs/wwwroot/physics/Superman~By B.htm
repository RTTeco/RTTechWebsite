Facts to know about this thrill ride:

Type of attraction: Reverse Freefall

Height: 415 Feet

Top Speed: 100 mph

Length: 1315 Feet

Acceleration: 0 to 100 mph in 7 seconds

Zero Gravity Freefall: 6.5 seconds

Ride Vehicles: 2 six Ton 15-Passenger Trains

Ride Designer: Intamin AG

Location: Six Flags Magic Mountain in Valencia, California
 
 
 
 

DREAMWORLD
 ~ A wonderful family theme park ~

This fun-filled theme park is located in Coomera, Australia.  It opened in December 15, 1981, and was "dreamed" up by John Longhurst.  Dreamworld has about 650 employees during the off-peak season and up to 1200 when it hits its peak period.
