The Flying

Super Saturator

By Nickelodeon

      The Flying Super Saturator ride made its debut on April 1, 2001 at Paramount's Carowinds in an area near the park's WaterWorks waterpark.  This Swingthing coaster features four seater cars that are suspended from an overhead track, allowing them to swing freely.  Riders sit back-to-back with their legs dangling while traversing the twisty, wet course.
