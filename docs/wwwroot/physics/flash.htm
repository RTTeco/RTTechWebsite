Flashbacks you will have but of what is the scary question!!!!

    The only roller coaster of its kind ever built, the Flashback will simulate the feeling and motion of a fighter jet rolling over and diving.

 

Designer: Intamin AG of Switzerland

Height: 86 feet

Length: 1,900 feet

Top Speed: 35mph

Features: Six diving drops, 720-degree upward helix.

Location:   Six Flags Magic Mountain

 

                Six Flags Magic Mountain         

HISTORY

   Six Flags Magic Mountain started with the desire of the Newhall Land and Farm company to build a park for their 'master planned community' of Valencia.  The plans were drawn up, and construction began in 1969, 2 years before the park would open to the public.  It had cost approximately 20 million dollars.  When the park opened on May 29th, 1971, there were 5oo employees and 33 attractions.  Today, in the height of the summer season, Magic Mountain employs over 3000 people.  Some of the rides that opened with the park were:  Gold Rush, Log Jammer, The Sky Tower, El Bumpo, Eagle's Flight, The Metro, and many others including some spinning "flat" rides.
