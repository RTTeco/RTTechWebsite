The Outlaw

The Outlaw is a wooden rollercoaster found at Adventureland in Des Moines, IA.  It is a twister kind of rollercoaster.  It was made in 1993 by Custom Coasters, Inc. 
Height:  70 feet Drops:  67 feet
Top Speed:  48 mph Length:  2,800 feet
Number of trains:  1 Designed by:  Philadelphia Toboggan Company
