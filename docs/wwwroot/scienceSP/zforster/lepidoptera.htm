LEPIDOPTERA

 

Lepidoptera have large, scaled wings, and chewing mouth parts in the larva stage. And a siphoning mouthparts in the adult form of life. The help Humans by pollinating flowers the early stages of their life such as the larva and pupa produce silk. But the main down fall of the Lepidoptera is that the larva damage clothing and crops.
