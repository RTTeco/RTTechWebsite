LEPIDOPTERA

Within the order Lepidoptera there are many characteristics that the insects share. The first trait is that the insects all have four separate wings, two on each side of the body. within  this order there is also complete and incomplete metamorphosis. The Lepidoptera order is made of moths and butterflies, these insects cocoon themselves for about two weeks, before they cocoon themselves they are caterpillars and after they break free of the cocoon they are either a moth or a butterfly. There are many different species of butterflies the most common being the monarch. There are also many species of moths, the largest species of moths would be the giant silkworm moth of Asia, it can have a wingspan of over 12 inches. The wingspan of the smallest moth are only a few millimeters. So as you can see for yourself  the order Lepidoptera have many various and interesting characteristics.

 Chocolate covered Crickets

Ingredients:

  • baker's chocolate
  • candied crickets

Directions:

Melt baker's chocolate in double boiler.

Fill molds halfway with chocolate, add grasshoppers, fill rest of the way.

A tasty surprise in every one!
