Characteristics :

There are more then 20,000 in this order they have a world wide distribution. Some characteristics are that they have a salutatory hind leg, most orthopterans have small and well separated hand coxae, a pronotum with large descending lateral lobes, nymphal wing rudiments reversing their orientation in later instars and hind tibiae with two dorsal teeth

  Chocolate Chirpie Chip Cookies

Ingredients:                                                         

2 1/4 cup flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 cup butter, softened
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
1 12-ounce chocolate chips
1 cup chopped nuts
1/2 cup dry-roasted crickets

Directions:

Preheat oven to 375. In small bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt; set aside. In large bowl, combine butter, sugar, brown sugar and vanilla; beat until creamy. Beat in eggs. Gradually add flour mixture and insects, mix will. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop by rounded measuring teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake for 8-10 minutes.

Fried Green Tomato Hornworms

Ingredients:

3 tablespoons olive oil
16 tomato horn worms
4 meduim green tomatoes, sliced into 1/4- inch rounds
Salt and pepper to taste
White cornmeal

Directions: In a large skillet or work, heat the oil. Then lightly fry the hornworms, about 4 minutes, taking care not to rupture the cuticles of each insect under high heat. Remove with a slotted spoon and set aside.

Season tomato rounds with salt and pepper, then coat with cornmeal on both sides.

In a large skillet, fry tomatoes until lightly browned on both sides.

Top each round with 2 fried tomato hornworms.

Garnish the paired hornworms with a single basil leaf.

Funis-Cornu

The Funis-Cornu uses it's roper like antennas to rope fly's and other insects as they fly by. All of its spike like horns is used as protection against spiders and all other predators. In the day time the funis-corun lives in the top tress and blends in with all of the colorful leaves, at night fall it sometimes goes down to the ground to feed on grasshopper eggs.
