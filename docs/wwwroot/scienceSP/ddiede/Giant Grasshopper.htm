Orthoptera

Characteristics

Created by: Dillon Diede

The name Orthoptera is sometimes used for all the insects in the "Orthopteroid". It is more usual to restrict the Orthoptera, Greek for "straight-wing", to the crickets, grasshoppers, and walking sticks. These insects are instantly recognizable by their long hind legs, which are modified for jumping. Most Orthoptera can make noise by rubbing special organs together on their legs or on their wings. Mole crickets rub this file over another specialized structure on the other wing, the "scraper," to make sound. Special membranes on the legs or on the abdomen allow Orthoptera hear these sounds, which are used in mating.

Orthoptera have salutatory hind legs, and small separated hind coxae, a pronotum with large descending lateral lobes, nymphal wing rudiments reversing their orientation in later instars and hind tibiae with two dorsal teeth rows. Other characteristics are unsegmented cerci and leathery forewings.

"Yummy Recipes"

Banana Worm Bread

Ingredients:

1/2 cup shortening

3/4 cup sugar

2 bananas, mashed

2 cups flour

1 teaspoon soda

1 teaspoon salt

1/2 cup chopped nuts

2 eggs

1/4 cup dry-roasted army worms

Directions:

Mix together all ingredients. Bake in greased loaf pan at 350 degrees for about 1 hour.

 

Mealworm Fried Rice

Ingredients:

1 egg, beaten

1 tsp. oil

3/4 c. water

1/4 c. chopped onions

4 tsp. soy sauce

1/8 tsp. garlic powder

1 c. minute rice

1 c. cooked mealworms+

Directions:

Scramble egg in a saucepan, stirring to break egg into pieces. Add water, soy sauce, garlic and onions. Bring to a boil. Stir in rice. Cover; remove from heat and let stand five minutes

 

Vas-gramen-spera -means "Giant Grasshopper"

My bugs order is the Orthoptera. It lives in the tropical rain forests of South America. It is about 6-8 inches long fully grown. My bug has the ability to change colors to blend into the surroundings. It also has fake eyes on its wings. When a nymph it molts until adulthood. It eats leaves and stems off of trees. It has many predators in the air but not much on the ground. The predators include birds and some snakes. My bug has a powerful jaw that it bits its predators with and it has the ability to fly away from danger.
