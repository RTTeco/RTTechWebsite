Diptera

    Diptera is one of the four largest groups of all living organisms. Their is 120,000 different types of flies known to man and is estimated that their is over 1,000,000different kinds. They are classified into 188 families. Diptera have hind wings that are reduced to small structures. the front wings are aerodynamic surfaces. All the larva are legless and live in freshwater environments.

Yummy Recipes

Mealworm Fried Rice

Ingredients:

·          1 egg, beaten

·          1 tsp. oil

·          3/4 c. water

·          1/4 c. chopped onions

·          4 tsp. soy sauce

·          1/8 tsp. garlic powder

·          1 c. minute rice

1 c. cooked mealworms

Directions:

Scramble egg in a saucepan, stirring to break egg into pieces.

Add water, soy sauce, garlic and onions. Bring to a boil.

Stir in rice. Cover; remove from heat and let stand five minutes.

Banana Worm Bread

Ingredients:

·          1/2 cup shortening

·          3/4 cup sugar

·          2 bananas, mashed

·          2 cups flour

·          1 teaspoon soda

·          1 teaspoon salt

·          1/2 cup chopped nuts

·          2 eggs

1/4 cup dry-roasted army worms

Directions:

Mix together all ingredients. Bake in greased loaf pan at 350 degrees for about 1 hour.

Aer Volaticus

The Air Fly

 

    Aer Volaticus is part of the order Diptera. It lives in tropical regions and most commonly is found in the Amazon rain forest. The one adaptive advantage that it has is front wings are longer then most members of the Diptera order, this blows air on the air fly and cools it off. The insect grows into an adult by complete metamorphosis. It eats most any kinds of plants but likes tree leaves. It defends itself by being able to fly fast because of it's long wings.
