The Richardton Memorial Hospital is located in two separate facilities (hospital and clinic facility) located on the north side of the Richardton community.  The hospital is a 25 bed critical access hospital licensed as a primary care hospital.  The clinic is certified as a rural health clinic.

Services include: acute care, observation care, swing bed (long term care in a hospital setting), respite care, 24-hour emergency room, rehabilitation services (physical, occupational and speech therapy), laboratory and radiology, two provider medical clinic, social services, nutritional services, fitness center, administrative and support services.

The Richardton Memorial Hospital creates an additional 30 jobs in the community and a total of 90 full and part-time employment positions.
