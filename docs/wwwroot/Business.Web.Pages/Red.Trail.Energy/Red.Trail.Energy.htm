This is the Red Trail Energy Plant

 

 

 

 

 

 

    Red Trail Energy helps the environment by converting corn into a type of gasoline which is call ethanol. Red Trail Energy wants to create an economic benefit for investors, local communities, and the state of North Dakota by converting natural resources and regional corn production into ethanol and beneficial by products.

These are the Distillers for the Ethanol

    These distillers help balance the ration that the cattle seem to like.  It also produces DGS, a highly nutritious animal feed ingredient. It is often used in beef cattle rations. Lots of farmers like this feed and come to Red Trail just to get it for their cattle.
