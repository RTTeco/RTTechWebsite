Palomar Pulsed Light System

Women and men everywhere battle with unwanted hair, visible veins, sunspots, age spots and acne.  But there is something that can help them.  It is called the Palomar Pulsed Light System.  Although not all of these imperfections can be treated with Palomar Pulsed Light Systems it can help with a lot of them.

Pulsed light systems work by emitting pulses of intense light into the problem area.  Any area of your body (except directly around the eyes) can be treated.  Palomar Pulsed Light treatments work up to ten times faster and with less discomfort than traditional laser treatments.
