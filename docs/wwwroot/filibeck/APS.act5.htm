Photoshop  

Activity 5 – Virtual Field Trip
(50 pts)  

  1. Make a photo of yourself on a Virtual Field Trip.  You must use at least 3 pictures to create into 1 Virtual Field Trip photo.
  1. Choose a picture of yourself.
  1. Choose a location to travel to from the list:
    1. Paris
    2. Devils Tower
    3. Sphinx
    4. Grand Canyon
    5. Temple
    6. Bridge
    7. River
  1. Choose an animal to insert from the list:
    1. Ducky
    2. Gorilla
    3. Zebra
    4. German Shorthair Dog
    5. Pronghorn
    6. Rhino
  1. Mesh all 3 pictures together to make 1 new picture.  Use the following techniques:
    1. Layers
    2. Edit/copy : Edit/paste
    3. Clone stamp
    4. Eraser & different brushes
    5. Lasso tool
    6. Free Transform
  1. Go to Save A Copy, make sure it is a JPEG file.  Insert into Publisher, resize if needed, type your name in a text box.
  1. Print in color, and hand in.
