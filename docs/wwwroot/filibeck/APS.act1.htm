Activity 1 – Saving a Picture & Inserting into Publisher
(20 pts)  

  1. Download the picture of yourself into Photoshop from your media card. Use the pencil tool and write your name somewhere on the photo of yourself. Save it to your Photoshop folder (make sure it is a JPEG).
  1. Download the picture of your object into Photoshop from your media card.  Use the paintbrush tool and paint something on the picture of your object.  Save it to your Photoshop folder. (make sure it is a JPEG)

  2. Open Publisher / insert both pictures / resize so that they both fit on the page, put a label box under each describing the picture.
