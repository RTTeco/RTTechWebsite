Vacation Trip Presentation

(140 Points)

 

Ø      You will create a slide show to present to the class on a vacation.  Plan a 5 day 4 night trip.

 

 

  1.  _____Must be a minimum of 8 slides(5 pts)

 

  1. _____ Slide Transition- choose a variety of types of transition to apply to your slides. (5)

 

  1. _____ Custom Animation - animate at least 1 object per slide.(10)

 

  1. _____ Sound Effects – need 3 minimum, locations of your choice.(15)

 

  1. _____ Backgrounds – must use a variety of different colors, fill effects, or pictures. (20)

 

  1. _____ Slide 1 - Intro/your name/picture/ why you picked your vacation spot (5 pts)

 

  1. _____ Slide 2 – Airline name/logo of airline/cost (10 pts)

 

  1. _____ Slide 3 - Rental Car/picture/cost/make & model(10 pts)

 

  1. _____ Slide 4 – Hotel /picture/cost/services of the hotel(10 pts)

 

  1. _____ Slide 5 - Itinerary – plan an activity for a day/details/picture/costs(10 pts)

 

  1. _____ Slide 6 - Itinerary – pick an activity for a day/details/picture/costs(10 pts)

 

  1. _____ Slide 7 - Restaurant – menu/logo or picture/cost(5 pts)

 

  1. _____ Slide 8 - Closing – summary of trip/total costs.(5 pts)

 

  1. _____ Speech.  Present your show to the class in a professional fashion. (10)

 

  1. _____ Printout – Print in Color, Handouts version, 9 slides per page, staple this sheet on the top
    to hand in when you give your speech.  It will not be graded without this step(10)
