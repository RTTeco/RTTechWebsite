PowerPoint – Stock Market Presentation

(140 points)

 

You will create a presentation on your stock market portfolio.

 

  1. _____Must be a minimum of 8 slides long (5)

 

  1. _____1 slide needs to show information on your total Portfolio values
         a.  ___ Show the total value of your portfolio for week 1 and the current week now (5)
         b.  ___ Action button – linked to your Excel stock file – give a brief talk over your file(10)

 

  1. _____1 slide needs to show the names of your 10 companies(15)

 

  1. _____1 slide needs to show a line graph or bar graph of your progress of each weeks total value (10)

 

  1. _____Pick your top 3 stocks, must have a slide of each company that contains the following:  (15)
    1. ___ a company logo or graphic
    2. ___ where is the corporate headquarters located
    3. ___ what does your company do
    4. ___ why you bought it
    5. ___ 1 of your companies need to show a graph of your choice (10)
    6. ___ 1 of your companies need to have an Action Button that links to it’s daily price web page. (20)

 

  1. _____1 slide needs to show your estimate or prediction of which of your stocks will be the #1 and #2 best stocks in your portfolio.  Based on market information (5)

 

  1. _____ 1 slide should be a summary or closing (5)

 

  1. _____ Include animation & transition (5)

 

  1. _____ Backgrounds.  Must have 3 slides where the background is a picture or graphic.(5)

 

  1. _____ Include some money related clip arts (5)

 

  1. _____ Pointer – use the pointer pen with a color on 1 slide to enhance your speech(10)

 

  1. _____ Speech presentation.  You must talk about your slide show.  Make sure you show all the action buttons and graphs(10)
