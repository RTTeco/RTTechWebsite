New German Attack
•March 1918 – Start of German attack
•April 1918 – Germans were 50 miles from Paris
•July 1918 – Second Battle of the Marne
•French, Moroccan & US, supported by 100’s of tanks pushed the Germans back over the Marne River
•Aug. 8, 1918 – Second Battle of the Somme
•Allies pushed the Germans back farther
•A million US troops poured into France and advanced toward Germany.
•Sept. 29, 1918 – Gen. Ludendorff said the war was lost, demanded the German gov’t ask for peace.
