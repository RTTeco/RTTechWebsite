Bolsheviks Seize Power
•Oct 1917 – Bolsheviks grew from 50,000 to 240,000
•They were in a position to claim power in the name of the Soviets
•Nov 6, 1917 – Bolshevik forces seized the Winter Palace (seat of the provisional gov’t)
•Publicly Lenin turned the power over to the Congress of Soviets
–The real power passed to a council headed by Lenin
–Bolsheviks renamed themselves the Communists
