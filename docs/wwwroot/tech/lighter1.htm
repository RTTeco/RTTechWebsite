Design Areas : Construction, Civil Engineering

Overview: Structures are supported by relatively small structural members that give great support. This is accomplished by making geometric shapes such as triangles, circles, columns, or rectangles from structural materials.

Objective: To learn the difference between materials and their properties.
                   To learn to use one type of bonding agent efficiently.
                   Work independently or cooperatively.
                   Work with only assigned materials.
                   Work within a given limit of time.  

Challenge: Build the tallest tower possible that will support a inflated balloon for 30 second or more using spaghetti and mashing tape as the structural material.

Limits:
    No use of power tools.
    Use only the materials given.
    You will not receive any more.
    You will not be allowed to start over.
    Tower must be free standing..
    Tower must stand for 30 seconds during the testing time period only.
    Tower construction and testing will take one period or less to of class time.

Materials: 
    Pieces of spaghetti
    Masking tape
    Building platform
    One Balloon

Evaluation:    A - 20 points -The tallest tower and it remained standing for 30 seconds.

                        B -18 points - The tower that measures over 75% of the tallest tower and remains standing for 30
                             seconds.

                        C - 17 points - The tower that measures over 50% of the tallest tower and remains standing for 30
                               seconds.

                        C - 16 points - The tower that remains standing at original height.

                        D - 13 points - The tower fell before the 30 second minimum time.

                        F - 11 points - Tried but no tower submitted.

                        F - 0 points - No tower submitted.
