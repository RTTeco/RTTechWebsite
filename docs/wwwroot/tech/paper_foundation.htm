Objective: To learn the difference between materials and their properties.
                   To learn to use one type of bonding agent efficiently.
                   Work independently or cooperatively.
                    To learn to work with a limited inventory.
                   Work with only assigned materials.
                   Work within a given limit of time.

Problem:   Given one sheet of standard paper (8.5 x11 inches) and 10 cm (4 inches) of clear tape, construct a foundation that will support the most amount of wieght.

Limits:         One day or less of planning.
   No use of power tools.
   Use only the materials given. You will not receive any more.
   You will not be allowed to start over.
    Foundation can not be shorter than 3 inches (8.72 cm)
    Foundation must be free standing. Foundation not be taped to the table or floor.
    Foundation must support the weight for 15 seconds during the testing time period only.
    When the foundation fails the last weight that is applied is not counted in the total weight.
    Foundation construction and testing will take one period or less to of class time.
    Work in groups of no more than two.

Given:             One sheet of standard paper 8.5 x 11 inches.
       Ten (10) cm of clear tape.
       Access to scissor, xacto knife.

Evaluation:   A - 20 points -The foundation that supported the most weight.
                      B -18 points -The foundation that supported 75% of the  foundation that supported the most weight.
                      C -17 points -The foundation that supported 50% of the  foundation that supported the most weight.
                      C -16 points -The foundation that supported 25% of the  foundation that supported the most weight.
                      D -15 points -The foundation that supported 10% of the  foundation that supported the most weight
                      D -13 points - The foundation supported less than 10% of the foundation that supported the most weight.
                      F - 11 points - Tried but no foundation submitted.
                      F - 0 points - No foundation submitted.
