1. Equipment is off limits to the students unless they are authorized by the instructor. Any behavior which inflicts upon another students right's and of property and safety will not be permitted. All students will be required to follow the classroom rules and guidelines according to the school's assertive discipline policy (see your agenda).

2. Students are expected to be sitting at their assigned seats at the beginning of class, with all material ready and needed for the days class period before the second bell has sounded. You are TARDY if the bell sounds and you are not seated and ready for class.

3. Students are expected to have their assignments and homework handed in on time.

4. Students are expected to show respect to the instructor or other students while they are speaking or offering pertinent information about the course. Students are not allowed to interrupt the educational process.

5. There will be zero tolerance for any harassment; verbal, sexual, or physical.

6. The Do Right Rule. It is assumed by the Industrial Technology Staff that all students know the difference between "right and wrong". You are expected to make the "right" decision in matters of behavior, discipline and academics. You are to be held accountable for willfully making the "wrong" decision.

A portion of your grade is based upon your conduct, punctuality, and work effort in these classes. Daily points will be subtracted from your work ethic and conduct grade if you choose not to abide by these rules.

If you do choose not to follow the above rules, something will happen.

You grade will be reduced.
You may be asked to leave the room.
There may be a conference with the instructor.
You may be sent to the principal.
You may serve detention after school in this building.
We may have a conference with your parent (s) or guardian.
