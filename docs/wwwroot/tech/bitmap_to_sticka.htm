Convert a black and white piece of clip art to a Dr Stika document.

Step 1:  Find your clip art. It must be black and white only. It also must be line copy only.

Step 2: Copy and paste the clip art to a CorelDraw document.

Step 3: Adjust the size.

Step 4: Select Convert to Bitmap.

Step 5: Choose Black and White

Step 6: Select Trace Bitmap.

Step 7: Choose By Outline.

Step 8: Close the Trace document.

Step 9: The traced vector graphic is now on top of the original clip art.

            Move it aside and you will see it.

Step 10: Delete the original unless you want to keep it for later.  

Step 11. Using the Pen Tool choose narrow line.

Step 12. Choose No Fill. Your image should now be a single line vector drawing.

Step 13: Copy and paste the traced vector into a Dr.Stika document.

Step 14: Cut the design out on the color of vinyl of your choice.  

To place clip art on a Dr. Stika document

Step 1. Research your clipart and save in a folder or desktop.

Step 2. Load Dr. Stika application.

Step 3 . Select Import and load your clipart.

Step 4. Use the darkness narrow to get your image the way you like.

Step 5. Now resize and print the image on the desired vinyl.
