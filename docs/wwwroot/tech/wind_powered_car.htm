Objective: To learn the difference between materials and their properties.
                   To learn to use a bonding agent efficiently.
                   To learn and recognize different types of friction.
                   Work independently or cooperatively.
                   Work with only assigned materials.
                   Work within a given limit of time.

Problem:   Given two sheets of standard paper (8.5 x11 inches), white glue, and clear tape, construct a vehicle that will roll the farthest distance. Using a 24 inch box fan as a power source.

Limits:          One day or less of planning (homework).
    No use of power tools.
    Use only the materials given.
    You will receive more if needed.
    You will be allowed to start over.
    Vehicle should be free wheeling.
    Vehicle should support itself.
    Vehicle construction and testing will take one period or less to of class time.
    Work in groups of no more than two.

Given:             One sheet of standard paper 8.5 x 11 inches.
       Set of four wheels with axels.
       Clear tape.
       White glue
       Access to scissor, xacto knife.

Evaluation A Best Design:
                        A - 20 points -The best designed vehicle.
                        B -18 points -The second best designed vehicle.
                        C - 17 points -The rest of the vehicles.
                        F - 11 points - Tried but no vehicle submitted.
                         F - 0 points - No vehicle submitted.

Evaluation B Best Performance:
                        A - 20 points -The vehicle that rolled the farthest distance.
                        B -18 points -The vehicle that rolled 75% of the farthest distance.
                        B -17 points -The vehicle that rolled 50% of the farthest distance.
                        B -16 points -The vehicle that rolled 25% of the farthest distance.
                        C -15 points -The rest of the vehicles.
                        F - 11 points - Tried but no vehicle submitted.
                         F - 0 points - No vehicle submitted.
