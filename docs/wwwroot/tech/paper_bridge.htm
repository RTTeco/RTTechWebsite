Objective: To learn the difference between materials and their properties.
                   To learn to use one type of bonding agent efficiently.
                   Work independently or cooperatively.
                   Work with only assigned materials.
                   Work within a given limit of time.

Problem:   Given one sheet of standard paper (8.5 x11 inches) and 10 cm (4 inches) of clear tape, construct a bridge that will span the longest distance.

Limits:          One day or less of planning (homework).
    No use of power tools.
    Use only the materials given. You will not receive any more.
    You will not be allowed to start over.
    Bridge must be free standing. Bridge not be taped to the supports.
    Bridge must stand for 30 seconds during the testing time period only.
    Bridge construction and testing will take one period or less to of class time.
    Work in groups of no more than two.

Given:             One sheet of standard paper 8.5 x 11 inches.
       Ten (10) cm of clear tape.
       Access to scissor, xacto knife.

Evaluation: A - 20 points -The longest bridge and it remained standing for 30 seconds.
                    B -18 points -The bridge that measures over 75% of the longest bridge and remains standing for                                                      30 seconds.
                     C - 17 points -The bridge that measures over 50% of the longest bridge and remains standing for 
                                                30 seconds.
                    C - 16 points - The bridge that measures over 25% of the longest bridge and remains standing 
                                                for 30 seconds.
                    C - 15 points - The bridge that measures over 10% of the longest bridge and remains standing for 
                                                30  seconds.  
                     D - 13 points - The bridge fell before the 30 second minimum time.

                      F - 11 points - Tried but no bridge submitted.

                       F - 0 points - No bridge submitted.
