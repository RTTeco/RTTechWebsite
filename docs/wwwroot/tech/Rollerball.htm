ROLLER BALL

Objective:        Demonstrate problem-solving skills. Use technology to solve problems. Use research and experimentation procedures to develop new processes and procedures. Work independently or cooperatively. Work with only assigned materials. Work within a given limit of time.

Problem:   Build a structure that will slow the descent of a marble in the longest time. A marble will enter the top left corner of the Roller Ball. The marble will exit the lower right corner of the Roller Ball. The marble that does this in the longest time will determine the remainder of the grade scoring.

Limits:      The structure is to be made entirely from paper, paper products or any material that has no value.
The structure will be no larger than a soda pop case flat or 16 x 20 inches.
The structure must be self-supporting.
The Roller Ball will have no mechanical mechanisms.
  Minimum material for legs, bracing, or any support material can be added to the outside of the Roller Ball.
Once the marble is placed in the entrance hole the Roller Ball cannot be touched.
One period for discussion and startup
The Roller Ball can be a homework assignment. Testing will begin the next class period.

Given:       One soda pop cardboard flat or equivalent.
                  One entrance and exit tube.
                  One marble

When the Roller Ball is finished and pre-tested. The Roller Ball marble will not be touched by anyone during the final timing and evaluation.

Evaluation:            A        -Structure that has the slowest descending ball

                                B        -Structure that has 75% if the slowest descending ball

                                C        -Structure that has 50% of the slowest descending ball

                                D        -Structure that has 25% of the slowest descending ball

                                F        - No structure submitted

Subjective judgment on uniqueness of designs and material used the instructor will award 1-10 points for creative solutions. This score will be added to the actual performance of the Roller Ball to determine the final score.
