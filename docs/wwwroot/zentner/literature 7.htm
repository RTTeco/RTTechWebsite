In this area of study, the students read various forms of literature for meanings of the various elements of each form.  These forms include short stories, poems, essays, novellas, and novels.

Each section of the literature series has a title which explains the theme of each section.  The following is a list of sections studied in Seventh Grade English:

The Valiant - Includes short stories on heroes of past and present, mythology studies, and the major work "The twelve Labors of Hercules".  The students also get the opportunity of creating their own mythological masks.

Changeover - Includes short stories on the changes that take place in lives of the characters.  The major work in this section is "A Christmas Carol".

Against Odds- This section includes stories of characters that have overcome obstacles in their lives.  "The Call of the Wild" is the major work in this section.

To Be Somebody is the section where characters try to make something of themselves though meeting hardships on the way.  Many short stories are read to explain this journey, and the theme is culminated in the work "Treasure Island".
