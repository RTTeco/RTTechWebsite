THE FIREMOUSE

 

Objective: To learn the difference between potential and kinetic energy. Work independently or cooperatively. Work with only assigned materials. Work within a given limit of time.

Problem:   Given the approved materials, extinguish a standard birthday candle with the potential and kinetic energy of a mousetrap placed one meter apart.

 

Limits:            One day of planning (homework).

                       Work in groups of no more than two.

                        Each group will have an allotted time limit to set up (no more than a class period).

                        Each group will have as many attempts as time allows or candle allows.

                        The candle must remain standing after it is extinguished.

                        Group will be limited to the material given only, no substitutes.

 

Given:             One base plate approximately 20.32cm x 111.76cm

                        One standard mousetrap

                        One birthday candle

                        One meter of string

                        White glue

                        One large soda straw

                        One small soda straw

                        Three 3” x 5” index cards

                        2 sheets of standard paper (8.5” x 11”)

                        Ten centimeters of tape, (masking or clear)

                        Hot glue only to secure mousetrap to base plate

 

The set up:        Mousetrap and candle are placed one meter apart on base plate.

 A hole may be drilled to fit the candle.

Mousetrap can be place in either direction toward the candle.

 

Evaluation:    A        -The flame is extinguished before ¼ of the candle is burned.

                        B        -The flame is extinguished before ½ of the candle is burned.

                        C        -The flame is extinguished before ¾ of the candle is burned.

                        D        - The flame is extinguished before the candle burns itself out.

                        F        - No Firemouse submitted.
