Choice of Bills

Place your picture on a piece of currency like the sample below.

 

Directions

Before beginning this project you must have a digital picture of yourself stored on a disk, on the hard drive, or in your web folder.

Step one:  Choose one of the bills on the next link.

Step two: Copy and the bill of choice by right clicking and selecting copy with your mouse.

Step three: Exit out or minimize the web site.

Step four: Open up a Microsoft PhotoDraw document, or a CorelDraw 9 document.

Step five: Copy the bill you choose to the document.

Step six: Insert your picture on the same page of the bill.

Step six and one half: If you are using CorelDraw, create an oval the same size as the white space on the bill

Step seven: Crop and cut your picture to fit  within the white space on the bill.

Step seven and one half: If you are using CorelDraw, Select your picture. Choose "Power Clip" from the Effects pull down menu. Choose inside container. Then click on the oval you created. Move your picture on the the bill's white space.

Step eight: Print your bill, and trim to size.
