Space
Space is the area of the paper or form that is refined by line. Two forms of space are positive and negative space.
                  Positive Space     Negative Space
Form
    Form is also defined by line. What space is encompassed by this line may take three different forms. One Dimensional (flat), Two Dimensional (flat on one side, not on other), and Three Dimensional (not flat, in-the-round, can look at the art piece from all angles such as a sculpture).
Review + Assignment
Reviewing the elements of design, we have to this point created a line design and defined shape of clothe and also gestures by use of line. We have also created color wheels including the primary, secondary, and intermediate colors. We have studied value by creating a line drawing and finishing this drawing with use of value shading. Details and textures were also created with this large drawing. We have transferred textures of our environment into a line design also. Your next assignment will be to create form with use of space and including texture, weather it be simulated or refined textures. Our terms: Create clay sculpture, thinking about the use of space, and adding in textures by tools and/or glaze.
Look for book/internet research examples of clay forms that include textures. Following research, draw a refined sketch of the form you will create out of clay. Decide what kind of tools will make the textures that you want to portray. Include texture in refined drawing. Hand in for preview.
