“Uncle Sam” is a kind of code or sign rather than an actual person and, like the Statue of Liberty, is a mythic image.  It stands for the United States of America—”U…S AM”.  The figure is a personification of the idea of the United States as a decisive nation, a nation that knows what it wants.  And Uncle Sam is coded by virtue of his outfit– a red-white-and-blue color scheme and stars on his hat band, which allude to the American Flag.
“WANT YOU” in reality means he wants you to risk you life for your country.  In other words, the image is appealing to your sense of patriotism and self-sacrifice.
This poster is a form of advertisement.  An art form of displaying a message to the reader.
