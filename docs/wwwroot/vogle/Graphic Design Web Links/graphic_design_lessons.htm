Graphic Design Homepage

 

Stages of Layout & Newspaper Comps
Reviewing newspaper/magazine advertisements for positive and negative design elements.
Principles & Elements of Design
Learn principles and elements of design through lecture, demonstration,  research, and practice.

Re-design newspaper comps in relation to principles and elements of design.
Social Ning Site
Understanding Social Discussion Networks. Demonstration and practice creating personal social sites using Ning as a host site. Students learn and practice professional manner of technology discussion through course discussion. Discussion relates to learned topics of Graphic Design, lecture topics, quiz questions, research, graphic design, mass communication, technology, and business.
Photo Techniques

DPI and Photo coloring techniques learned. Photoshop used to manipulate photos for best quality according to dpi needs through print or online sources.

Photo Retouch Methods

Using Photoshop to fix photo mistakes, rips, watermarks, red-eye, and more.

Photo Filters

Photoshop filter methods manipulated to create unique photo work.

Drop-in/Drop-out Photo

Combining Photos to create a unique photo. Example: cutting a person from one photo and adding it to another.

Typography

Following Typography History Research: Lecture, Demonstration, Thumbnail Sketching, Comps, Photoshop Problem Solving will be incorporated for understanding in creating unique typography style.

Logo Design

Practice of Design Methods according to business setting, independently creating a logo design for a business or personal aspect.

Logo Package Design

Creating business card and letterhead as part of the logo package.

CD Design

Using Photoshop grid method, students create a CD design for front, back, and CD title.

Design Critique

Practice of writing skills regarding elements and principles of design in connection with advertisements and artwork.

Package Design

Creating package design using Photoshop techniques.

Photoshop Digital Effects

Creating digital effects in Photoshop for Web Page Design

Photo Montage

Using photo methods, Photoshop techniques, and creative sketching to create photo montage. Photo Montage will represent chosen theme.

Menu Design

This design project is a group project where students work together to create menu design according to chosen menu theme such as Coffee Shop, Smoothies, Restaurant, or other Theme. Students use sketching, camera, typography, and Photoshop to create menu design.

Newsletter

The newsletter is set up to represent a research project of student choice. Student will choose a topic to research. Student will represent this research topic through newsletter design including writings, photos, and examples.

Game Design

Students use drawing methods, photos, & Photoshop to create Game Design.

Semester Presentation

Students prepare and present reflection paper of learned methods, possible interests, uses, and growth of learning throughout Graphic Design and post it to social Ning site.
