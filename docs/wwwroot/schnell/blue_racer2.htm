Blue Racer

 

 

 

Description of the Blue Racer Snake: Length is 6 feet long, very skinny, has a whip like tail and a long head, big eyes and its skin is scaly.

It is either the colors blue or black and

Food: include rats, small animals such as insects and mice and drinks lots of water.

Breeding: In the springtime and lays around 12 eggs in decaying wood and under rocks.

Environment: Can be found in Southern Ontario

Summer live in open areas, like to have lots of cover such as wooded areas and are usually found in trees or in the grass.

Also they need lots of space to move around.
