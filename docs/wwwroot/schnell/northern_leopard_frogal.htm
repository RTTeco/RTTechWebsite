Northern Leopard Frog

  

Northern Leopard Frog

Environment:  Edges of Lakes and Rivers, marshes, and wet meadows

Description: Body Length 2-3 ½ inches, Breeding Period March-May, Young Per Year

3000 – 6000 per year, food insects, worms and spiders, the body is green and covered in black “leopard spots”, Life expectancy 3 years, Hibernates at the end of October and finds a place at the bottom of the lake or pond.

Range of the Northern Leopard Frog

Origin of the Northern Leopard Frog would be the bullfrog, the pickerel frog, and the green frog.
