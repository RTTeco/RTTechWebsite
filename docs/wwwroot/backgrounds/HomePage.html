Mission Statement:

Richardton Public School District #4 will provide the educational opportunities necessary for every student to achieve and succeed within his/her capacity to learn and progress.  These educational opportunities will stress the necessity of lifelong learning for each student and make every effort to prepare the student for the challenges of the 21st century.

About Richardton-Taylor High School

Richardton Public Schools merged with Taylor in 1983 and today 180 students in grades 7-12 attend school in Richardton with 150 K-6 students at Taylor. Richardton-Taylor High School has been a leader in computer and technology classes along with strong programs in all curricular areas.  Every classroom is on the school computer network with internet access for all teachers and students.
