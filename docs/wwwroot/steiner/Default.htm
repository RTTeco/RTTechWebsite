Welcome to the Richardton-Taylor High School Special Services website.  The intention of this website is to provide a wealth of special education services and supports for students, parents, teachers and administrators.

West River Special Services is the central office for Richardton-Taylor Schools Special Services.  The primary responsibility of Special Services is to administrate programs and projects related to free appropriate public education of all children with disabilities, from the ages of 3 through the age of 21. West River Special Services

West River Special Services provides services to students under the following categories:

Specific Learning Disability (Reading Comprehension, Written Expression, Listening Comprehension, Mathematics Calculation, Basic Reading Skills, Mathematics Reasoning, and Oral Expression), Speech/Language Impaired, Mental Retardation, Emotional Disturbance, Hearing Impaired, Deaf-Blindness, Orthopedic Impairment, Traumatic Brain Injury, Autism, Visual Impairment, Deafness, Multiple Disabilities and Other Health Impaired (OHI)--In accordance with IDEA-and least restrictive environment, inclusion principles.
