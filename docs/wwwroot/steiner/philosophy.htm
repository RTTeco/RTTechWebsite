   RTHS Mission Statement 

Richardton-Taylor Public School District # 4 will provide the educational opportunities necessary for every student to achieve and succeed within his/her capacity to learn and progress. These educational opportunities will stress the necessity of lifelong learning for each student and make every effort to prepare the student for the challenges of the    21st century.
