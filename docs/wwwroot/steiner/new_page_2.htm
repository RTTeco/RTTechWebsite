Mrs. Steiner's Resource Room Discipline Policy

     Richardton-Taylor Assertive Discipline Policy

Classroom Rules

1.  All students will adhere to the rules and regulations as specified in their agendas.

2.  Students will be in the resource room with all work needed, before the bell rings.

3.  Students will use their time wisely and work throughout the period.

4.  Students will respect others in the classroom, including other classmates, their teachers and guests.

Consequences

1.  Verbal warning--to student and/or group of students.

2.  Movement to another area of the resource room.

3.  30 minutes detention with instructor on a solution to the problem.  (The principal will be informed about conduct).

4.  Removal from the resource room and contact will be made to parent of conduct.

{Positive Reinforcement)

1.  The student will receive verbal praise whenever suitable.

2.  The student will  be provided with a positive learning environment.

3.  Rewards will be given on special occasions.
