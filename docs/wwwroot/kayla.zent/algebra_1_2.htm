Algebra 1/2

Algebra ½ covers all topics normally taught in pre-algebra, as well as additional topics from geometry and discrete mathematics (used in engineering and computer sciences).

With Algebra ½ , students can deepen their understanding of pre-algebraic topics.

Algebra ½ includes:

  • instruction and enrichment on such topics as compressions,
  • approximating roots,
  • polynomials,
  • advanced graphing,
  • basic trigonometry,
  • and more.
